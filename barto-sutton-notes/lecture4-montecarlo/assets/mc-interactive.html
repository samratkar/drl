Key Observation: In Monte Carlo, we first generate the entire episode, then compute returns backwards, then update Q-values. Unlike TD methods, MC must wait until the episode ends. In off-policy mode, watch how the importance sampling ratio ρ scales the return — amplifying it when π would have taken the same actions more often than b did.
